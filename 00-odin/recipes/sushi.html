raw fish on rice

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, often paired with seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It comes in various forms, such as rolls, nigiri, or sashimi, and is typically served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for added flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Fish or seafood
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice:

    2. Prepare sushi rice by cooking short-grain rice, then mix it with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let it cool to room temperature.

  2. Prepare the fillings:

    3. Slice fish, seafood, and vegetables into thin, even strips or pieces.

  3. Lay the nori

    4. Place a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on a bamboo sushi mat or clean surface.

  4. Add rice and fillings:

    5. Spread a thin, even layer of rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top. Add fish and vegetables in a line near the bottom edge.

  5. Roll the sushi:

    6. Use the bamboo mat to tightly roll the nori into a log, pressing gently as you go to secure the roll.

  6. Slice and serve:

    7. Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife, and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
