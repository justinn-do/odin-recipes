pasta, sauce

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with fillings such as meat, cheese, vegetables, and rich tomato or béchamel sauce, then baked to golden perfection. It is a hearty, flavorful comfort food often served in large portions, making it perfect for gatherings and family meals.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat and sauce:

    2. Brown the ground meat, then mix it with tomato sauce and seasonings.

  2. Prepare the cheese mix:

    3. Combine ricotta (or cottage cheese), shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan in a bowl.

  3. Cook the pasta

    4. Boil lasagna sheets until tender, then drain and set aside.

  4. Layer the lasagna:

    5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom, then layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat until the dish is full, ending with a cheese layer on top.

  5. Bake:

    6. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 10-15 minutes to brown the top.

  6. Rest and serve:

    7. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
